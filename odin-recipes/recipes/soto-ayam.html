Ingredients

The Chicken and Soup Base

  1. 8 x Chicken Thighs skins removed
  2. 8 cups of chicken stock no sodium
  3. 1 x Lemongrass stalk cut into three pieces
  4. 3 x Kaffir lime leaves
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. vegetable oil to shallow fry

The Spice paste

  1. 1 cup of shallots chopped
  2. 5 x cloves of garlic chopped
  3. 1 inch fresh ginger chopped
  4. 1 tbsp of candlenuts
  5. 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder
  6. 1 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  7. 1/2 tsp pepper
  8. 1/2 cup coconut milk
  9. 3 tbsp vegetable oil

The Soto Ayam

  1. The stock from cooking the chicken
  2. 12 oz rice vermicelli noodles
  3. 4 large eggs
  4. chopped cilantro to garnish
  5. fried shallots to garnish
  6. sambal oelek to serve
  7. lime wedges to serve

Instructions

Prep the Chicken

  1. Skin the chicken
  2. Combine the stock, kaffir lime leaf, lemongrass and salt in a pot large enough to hold all the ingredients. Bring to a simmer
  3. Add the chicken and simmer until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 170F. This should take 15-20 minutes, don't boild. Simmer with little bubbles
  4. Remove the chicken from the broth. Blot dry. Set aside. You can also turn off the heat under the broth for now

Fry the chicken

  1. If you fried shallots just keep going with the chicken. If not heat around 2 cups of il in a frying pan large enough to hold half the chicken (4 thighs). A spatter guard isn't a bad idea here if you have one
  2. Fry on one side for about 2-3 minutes. Flip the chicken and cook another 2-3 minutes. You want the chicken to brown but you don't want to cook it to death.. Remove the chicken and repeat with the other 4 thighs. Set aside to cool enough to handle
  3. When it's cool enough pull the chicken off the bone and shred it

Medium boil the eggs

  1. Bring enough water to cover the eggs by one inchg to a boil in a small saucepan. Add the eggs and set a timer. You want to cook them exactly seven minutes
  2. After seven minutes remove them from the pan and submerge in an ice bath. This stops the egg from cooking more. This should get you about a perfect medium boiled egg. peel but don't slice them yet.

Assemble the soto ayam

  1. Bring the broth to a lively simmer. Taste it. Be careful. It's hot. It will need more salt. It will be somewhere around another 1/2 to full teaspoon. maybe more, season to your taste
  2. To soften the rice vermicelli just fully submerge it in the hottest water you can get out of your tap Let it sit five minutes and drain. Rinse with cold water. People that tell you to cook rice mermicelli like to eat rice noodle mush
  3. Have your garnishes ready. Slice your eggs in half, use a sharp knife and be careful. Those yolks are still soft.
  4. To serve place 1/4 of the rice noodles in each of 4 bowls. Divide the chicken and place it on the vermicelli. Top with fried shallots and cilantro. Pour 2 cups of broth into each bowl. Add one egg and a slice of lime per bowl. Serve. Add sambal oelek to taste. Enjoy
